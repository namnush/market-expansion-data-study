# market-expansion-data-study# Market Expansion Data Study

## Overview

This project analyzes whether Eniac should partner with **Magist** for expansion into the Brazilian market.

Using SQL analysis and Tableau visualizations, this study evaluates Magist’s operational performance, delivery reliability, customer satisfaction, and suitability for premium technology products.

The final recommendation was developed through data analysis, business evaluation, and visualization storytelling.

---

## Business Problem

Eniac is considering expanding into Brazil through a partnership with Magist.

### Main Question

> Can Magist support Eniac’s high-end technology products while maintaining delivery quality and customer satisfaction?

---

## Objectives

- Analyze Magist’s marketplace performance
- Evaluate delivery speed and delays
- Investigate customer satisfaction trends
- Examine technology product sales volume
- Assess whether Magist aligns with Eniac’s premium brand positioning

---

## Key Findings

- Technology product sales represented a relatively small portion of total sales.
- Premium tech categories generated limited revenue.
- Delivery delays could negatively affect customer experience.
- Customer reviews revealed operational inconsistencies.
- Magist’s infrastructure appeared more suitable for mass-market products rather than premium electronics.

---

## Final Recommendation

Magist is **not an ideal strategic partner** for Eniac’s premium technology expansion due to operational limitations and insufficient support for high-end tech products.
